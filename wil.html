The fluffy little bunny sat in the middle of the garden, its twitchy nose wiggling with excitement. With wide eyes and eager paws, it dove into a pile of fresh vegetables like a tiny, furry vacuum cleaner. Carrots disappeared in seconds, followed by crunchy lettuce and a few daring bites of a ripe tomato. Its soft ears flopped forward every time it chewed, making the whole scene look like a slow-motion snack attack. By the end of its feast, the bunny’s round belly poked out adorably, and it flopped onto its side with a satisfied sigh, as if to say, “That was delicious.”
